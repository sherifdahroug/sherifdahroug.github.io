Ëirene - Old comedy!

Music by Sherif DAHROUG



Eirene -Old comedy! With a Bi-tonal, quasi new age Fantasia, comes the two mouvements Eirene -Old comedy! to sound design Aristophanes' abstruse play. A music offering, composed, designed and performed by Sherif DAHROUG. EP released November 2023 with the "Lyre de mercure" french Label. In his masterpiece Eirene, The Greek playwright Aristophanes narrates the scene of Hermes entering Olympus to find that the Greek gods of the Olympus had abandoned the noble mountain. Once again, the divin mountain was inhabited by devastation, pain, terror, dread, and all the gods that embody conflict and separation. The earth was no longer anything but a mirror of this tragic devastation. Humans abandoned the gods, and the gods abandoned Olympus. Destruction and war buried and imprisoned Eirene in a deep well, so there is no light nor life. Eyes and hopes pray to the harp of peace and wisdom that will separate light from darkness. Eirene -Old comedy! A two-mouvements artwork that relies on the influence of the Bi-tonal harmony to symbolise the duality that describes heaven and earth, war and peace, to extract more human moments amidst the absurdity of fate.
