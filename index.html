Recording Academy / GRAMMYs®

In 2025, Sherif Dahroug was officially invited to join the Recording Academy / GRAMMYs® as a Voting Member (Class of 2025) — a distinction extended in recognition of his artistic vision and his contribution to the evolution of contemporary music on today’s global musical landscape. An opportunity to take part in a collective effort dedicated to honoring musical creation and supporting its evolution on a global scale.

New Book Announcement

New Book: "In Pursuit of a Sonic Architectonics"

Dive into a Bold Reflection That Transcends Classical Aesthetic Boundaries In Pursuit of a Sonic Architectonics neither seeks to conform to a rigid theoretical tradition nor to dismiss it. Its ambition? To explore beauty in its contradictions and dissonances, bridging contemplative reflection, with its transcendental dimensions, and the immanent demands of technical realization. This book embarks on a rare and daring exercise, aiming for timeless universality while remaining deeply contextual—rooted in a symbolic and phenomenological interpretation. Drawing its essence from Egyptian philosophy, a millennia-old tradition of regeneration, it shapes an aesthetic where abstraction and mystery merge into an eternal language. Like art itself, In Pursuit of a Sonic Architectonics creates a unique and intimate universe—a space of artistic liberation where every enigma becomes a suspended answer. Far from claiming to unravel the mysteries of its architectonic cosmos or dispel the philosophical aura of sonic art, it amplifies the mystery itself. This work stands as a profound and thoughtful alternative to the cultural alienation of our era. Above all, In Pursuit of a Sonic Architectonics is a manifesto for an autonomous, liberated, and resolutely timeless sonic aesthetic. It is a vibrant call to reconnect with the roots of art and life, where the horizon of sonic creation transforms into a boundless territory. In its quest for a new artistic vision, this book is both an invitation to exploration and a declaration for those daring enough to dream beyond established conventions.
