Honour · Paris

Une distinction tenue dans la lumière.

The annual awards ceremony marked a major artistic recognition: the conferral in Paris of the Médaille de Platine by the prestigious Société Académique Arts-Sciences-Lettres, the highest distinction within the institution’s hierarchy of honours, awarded under the High Patronage of the President of the French Republic and recognized by the Académie Française for exceptional contributions to art, science, and letters. The distinction honours a singular body of work in which these disciplines converge once more, reuniting worlds too often held apart and opening a rare dialogue between artistic creation, philosophical thought, and scientific imagination.

In Pursuit of a Sonic Architectonics

A bold reflection that transcends classical aesthetic boundaries: neither conforming to a rigid theoretical tradition nor dismissing it, but exploring beauty through contradiction, dissonance, contemplation, and technical realization. Rooted in symbolic and phenomenological interpretation, the book proposes an autonomous, liberated, and timeless sonic aesthetic.
