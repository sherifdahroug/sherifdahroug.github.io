Distinction

Platinum Prize — World Classical Music Awards

London 2025 — awarded for the album and original composition Dichotomy.

Receiving the Platinum Prize at the World Classical Music Awards — London 2025, for the album and original composition Dichotomy, marks a moment of profound gratitude and quiet responsibility.

From the first sketch to the final vision and composition, from recording and performing to post-production, artwork, and the accompanying philosophical book, Dichotomy has been a dedicated journey that few can truly imagine.

It began in a deep inner silence; listening for the first notes that would one day unfold into an entire structure. It is a path that humbles even the most accomplished musician, reminding us that creation is always larger than ourselves.
