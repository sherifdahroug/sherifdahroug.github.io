Global Music Awards — 2026

Silver Medal Recognition

Dichotomy — A Tone Poem of Five Metamorphoses
Composition / Composer · Official announcement: February 2, 2026 · La Jolla, California, USA

In this season of global musical celebration, my album Dichotomy – A Tone Poem of Five Metamorphoses has been honored with a Silver Medal at the Global Music Awards (Composition / Composer), officially announced on February 2, 2026, in La Jolla, California, USA.

This distinction is received with gratitude, having stood among thousands of remarkable albums and projects by accomplished musicians of our time and orchestras of lasting distinction. While no award can truly gratify any album—especially those born from original, newly created music, a new language, and a profound concept—it can honor the devotion, love, and countless hours poured into the work, from composition and conceptualization, to interpretation and performance, through long recording sessions and post-production, in order to offer this music to the world. In this sense, the celebration remains a symbolic gesture.

This honor is humbly received as part of a journey of new creations and explorations. In this spirit, it also seeks—however modestly—to contribute to redefining the international community, especially in a time marked by a widening gap between true independent excellence and the growing sense of shallowness often driven by commercial value within large industries and mass consumption.

Keep creating.
