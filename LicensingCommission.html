Music for Culture, Heritage, and Film

Original Compositions by Sherif Dahroug – Available for Licensing & Commission

Sherif Dahroug’s music bridges continents, centuries, and philosophies. Rooted in Egyptian heritage yet viewed through a refined contemporary lens, his works evoke the grandeur of history, the intimacy of human stories, and the abstraction of timeless thought. Each composition offers a distinctive narrative layer—perfect for documentaries, heritage films, museum exhibitions, and cultural projects—forming an architecture of sound and space that resonates beyond time.

Themes & Storylines

  • Ancient Civilizations & Their Philosophies – Music inspired by the wisdom, myths, and spiritual depth of early cultures.
  • The Architecture of Sound & Space – Compositions that shape sonic landscapes as if sculpting a temple for the ear.
  • Cross-Cultural Dialogues in Music – Bridging traditions to create new narratives that transcend borders.
  • Human Resilience & Transformation – Soundtracks to the timeless story of endurance, change, and rebirth.
  • Abstract & Avant-Garde Expressions – Explorations where form dissolves into pure sound and emotion.
  • Cosmic Events & Celestial Cycles – Musical reflections on the rhythm of the stars, planets, and universal order.
  • Nature, Creation, & the Forces of Life – Works that capture the vitality and mystery of the natural world.
  • Purism of Form & Expression – Stripped to its essence, music that speaks in a language of clarity and truth.
  • Innovation in Sound Design – Exploring new textures, timbres, and sonic possibilities.
  • Experimental Soundworlds – Pushing the boundaries of perception through unconventional sonic exploration.
  • Music for Contemporary Ballet & Performance Arts – Works composed for movement, space, and modern choreography.
  • Music for Meditation – Through a refined, studied, and metaphorical harmonic structure, shaping time and space into an introspective journey.
  • Music for Museums, Art Galleries & Fine Arts – Soundscapes for paintings, sculptures, and installations, transforming visual art into music.
  • Music for Theatrical Narrative – Original scores designed as a narrative backbone, weaving music into the core of storytelling.

How to Collaborate

  • License Existing Works – Ready-to-use tracks for immediate integration into film and exhibitions.
  • Commission Original Music – Bespoke scores tailored to specific projects.

Artist Statement

Artist Statement

Each commissioned work I undertake is approached with deep study and intention, seeking to capture the singular essence and purpose behind every project. Guided by the timeless principles of sacred geometry, my compositional process becomes a ritual—an architecture of sound where every interval, motif, and silence is placed with the precision of stones within an ancient temple.

Drawing upon my foundation in naval architecture and rigorous musical training, I see composition as both precise engineering and poetic ceremony. This disciplined synthesis allows me to design structures of sound that transcend conventional tonality, creating spaces where music becomes a vessel for metaphysical reflection and emotional resonance.

Rooted in traditions where art and spirituality converge, my aim is to craft music that not only aligns with your vision but also carries an enduring presence—bridging past and future to answer the present moment’s deepest call.

“From music is born the silent logos of the cosmos; each note echoes its architecture.”
— S. Dahroug
